Drum

I have seen a lot of big bands record the real drum in their recording. Does it sound better than the plug in? It would be depend. I think you have to have experience with the drum to have a good sounding. If you are solo artist, most home studio owner are, it would be hard to learn every structment. You can easier learn how to use the plug in to have a good drum sound. There are a lot of drum program that you can buy and use it as style you like. For example, EZ drumer 2 really has good sounding drum.
