Home Studio Desk

Do we really have to have studio desk to set up home studio? I think it depends. Like studio monitors, you can do the recording without it. It is not like computer or software. You can still make music without the studio desk. You can still put the computer on the bed or on the floor and recording the sound. But, if you would like a little set up, organizing, look more similar a studio, you should have the desk. You can place your monitor, computer, and interface on it.


Do you really have to buy the studio desk? No, you don't have to. First, the studio desk is expensive. Since the desk is not really necessary to make music, it doesn't have to be a fancy desk or expensive. Third, you can even build yourself if you have time to do it.
