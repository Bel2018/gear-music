Headphone

Headphone, the same as studio monitors, they are important. Like studio monitors, it is for you hear back what you are recording. But, the headphone is specifically important when you record vocal or other instruments Because you need to hear the clicks or music from your track to sing so that you are on the same tempo with tracks. You need to hear them from the headphone. If you turn on the monitor volume, your mic would capture all the noise, unnecessary sound.
