Midi Keyboard

Why do you want to have a midi keyboard? Do you play keyboard or you just want to it on your studio desk to look nice. It is all up to you. As it is always not bad to have a midi keyboard on studio desk, and it is not a problem to not have it either. It would be easier for those who play piano because they can recording directly from the midi when they record. But, you should be able to the piano and it costs when you buy it. But, if you want to have some string in your demo, but can't play, you have to work with the plug in and insert the notes in midi track by clicking around with your mouse.
