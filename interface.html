Audio Interface

You can start buying the equipment as you are working on setting up your studio. Not any specific equipments has to be first or the last to collect. You can buy them all the same time if you have a good budget to spend. But, if you are like me, I save little by little and buy the things that I need the most to begin studio and continue saving and continue buying to complete my studio project.


So if you already have your computer and software, you may install the software in your computer and ready to run. But, in order to hear you music or plug in with microphone or guitar, you need the audio interface. Then, you may able to record it.


Here are some of the example for the audio interface:

  • UAD Apllo Twin
  • Focusrite Sarlett 2i2
  • Audient iD14
  • Arturia Audio Fuse
  • PreSonus Quantum 2
  • Behringer U-Phoria UM2
