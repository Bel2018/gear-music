Microphone

Microphone is part of a big thing in musician production. It is simple because you need to recording you singing. It is not just reading, but you may the microphone in a lot of things in recording such as recording your voice, directly from guitar amplifier cabinet.


There are two main kind of microphones: condemnser and dynamic. They are both useful in particular place. For example, some people may suggest the condemser recording and use dynamic for capturing from guitar. But, who says what is the right way to use. You just have to use it to experience it by using the dynamic for vocal and also use both dynamic and condemser for micing up for the guitar tracks. You may find your best ways of using them.
