Acoustic Treatment

If you have home studio, I think you have to treat your room better. What does it mean? Which mean you need to put some acoustic panels on the wall or on ceiling. The main purpose is to absorb the unnecessary that your room creat when you recording. If you capture the crazy noise, it may be hard for you to edit your mix later. You should not wait to start and record untill you put up your panels though. You can start and treat your room better and better in time.
